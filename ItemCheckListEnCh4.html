Symbiogenesis Season 4 Item Check List

Item Name Check Box Caption
World Tree Talisman -For- A talisman that symbolizes the act of ”banding together.” One wonders what the dragon must think when it observes the flowers of the World Tree bloom and fall in quick succession.
Incomplete Generator (CH4) An uncharged generator, dormant and non-functional. Those who possess it can envision a new world.
Merchant Union-Certified Guard Emblem An emblem worn on the chest by Guards deemed competent by the Merchant Union. Owners of this item are subject to incessant requests for help.
Antique: Wild Oul A bottle of the famous Wild Oul whiskey, aged for several centuries. There isn't a person alive who wouldn't hesitate before opening the cap.
Navy Blade -Stormcaller- A sword presented by a master craftsman to the Twelve Blademasters of the Government during the Six Powers Era. Legend says it once cut a towering wave into two as it rose from the stormy seas.
Kraken's Eye The eye of a rarely-seen kraken. Considered one of three famous delicacies of the Floating Continent, it has a pleasantly bitter taste and melts on the tongue. Its unique mouthfeel soon captivates those who try it.
Ace Pendant A pendant worn by a legendary gambler who lived in Vegas City under the Company during the Six Powers Era. A mysterious button lies at its center.
Great Detective's Pipe An antique pipe once used by a famous detective of the past. Due to his influence, pipe-smoking has become synonymous with detective work.
Guard's Armband (B Grade) An armband created within the Guild at the Penal Colony. It presents the Guards who wear it as capable and trustworthy.
Chemical Caviar A processed food that was born out of an effort to make luxury goods accessible. However, the manufacturing costs involved are high, and mass production is yet to begin.
Circle Railway Service Kit A set of tools that are found on every railroad maintenance worker. From pipe leaks to loose screws, this kit will allow you to deal with all sorts of problems.
Flying Jet Pack: Prototype A prototype of a jet pack made by Miners. It uses World Tree Energy to create an explosive level of propulsion—a characteristic that makes it very hard to control.
Grilled Cheese Sandwich A sandwich created to increase the sales of cheese produced by the Normies in Research Village. The sight of oozing cheese is enough to stir anyone's appetite.
Miner-made Pen Spy Camera A spy tool in the shape of a pen, useful for performing intelligence assessments. It is generally used from the breast pocket, and the videos it takes can be sent instantly to a separate device.
Blue Topaz Ring A ring adorned with a blue gemstone that can be found in the Departed Land. Contains an inscription on its inner side that reads: "For Task, who forever has my love."
Treasure Map A map that would excite any Treasure Hunter that came across it. The avenues it presents have mostly been exhausted, but it's too early to give up hope yet.
Commemorative Coin Bolo Tie A bolo tie that places an extreme emphasis on the commemorative coin at its center. It was created by a certain Merchant who wanted to show off his prized possession.
Wealthy Merchant's Fountain Pen A fountain pen belonging to Steve, a Merchant who made a name for himself in the Company's York City during the Six Powers Era. His name is inscribed into the side.
Gold Aloe Perfume A perfume naturally derived from Gold Aloe. It has a faint sweet scent, and is considered to be appropriate for important business meetings.
Post-Vegas Sightseeing Guide A sightseeing guide that claims to have everything you need to know about Post-Vegas inside. Reading its pages will no doubt enhance your trip to the city.
Gold Chess Piece of the First Conservator A chess piece modeled after the First Conservator. It was created during his lifetime, and exists alongside other pieces depicting important figures of the time. A must-have item for any avid collector.
Painting: Sacred Tree Among the Skyscrapers A landscape painting created with meticulous care. It seems to depict a giant tree, reminiscent of the World Tree, standing amid several high-rise towers—a sight that evokes a sense of harmonic dichotomy.
The Floating Continent Monthly -Occult Edition- A magazine that features the occult for its tenth anniversary. It contains articles about undead Sprites, eerie experiments in Academy City, and Slasher Guards in the Penal Colony.
Research Village Canelés A pastry currently popular in Post-Vegas, created using the mellow-tasting milk of Research Village and high-quality sugar with a striking sweetness. Its appearance has also received high praise.
Mirror of Fortune A mirror said to increase the luck of those who groom themselves in its reflection daily. Unfortunately, it has no such effect, but the immoral Merchants knowingly continue to profit greatly from this.
Research Village's Famous Three-Color Dango A famous confectionery sold by a tea house in Research Village. Apparently, the green section represents fertile land; the pink, flowers of the World Tree; and the white, drifting clouds.
Plantation Coconut Spirit A spirit made by fermenting coconut juice. While it has a refreshing sweetness that makes it easy to drink, its alcohol content is quite high. Caution is advised.
ITO Bread A loaf of bread sold by ITO Foods. Its nutritional content is extremely high, as it's made using volcanic mineral water and various types of fruit juice.
Modified Stockyard Work Boots A pair of work boots worn by Guards at the Stockyard. It seems their soles have been modified to allow something to be hidden within—a feature that's often used for nefarious purposes.
Owl Feather Pen A pen made with the feather of an owl that inhabits the forest near the farmlands. It's praised for its ease of use, but lacks in durability, causing it to break easily.
Coconut Hammer A metal hammer used to crack open coconuts and other fruit with hard shells. It's not in the shape of a claw hammer—instead, one of its sides is pointed so it can create a breach.
Fishing Rope A line of rope used daily in the fishing industry. Sold under the guarantee that it will last 50 years without snapping.
Rough Floating Continent Map A copy of a map depicting the Floating Continent as it was soon after its discovery. While extremely old, it's still considered largely accurate to this day.
Nile-Certified Stomach Medicine A medicine for the stomach developed and sold independently by the Nile Trading Company. Merchants often use this before business negotiations to soothe their stress-induced stomach aches.
Penal Colony Moonshine A hearty spirit created by Guards in the Penal Colony. Its production forgoes certain complicated processing measures, allowing it to be cheaper than other drinks on the market.
Circle Railway Ticket Book A ticket book that is required for the Circle Railway—indispensable to those who work in Post-Vegas and sightseers. Each stop will cost you one ticket.
Circle Railway Stamp Book A book that has space for stamps offered by each station on the Circle Railway. It contains slots for non-existent stations, but these have plans to be built in the future.
Fleeting Petal Amulet An amulet that is considered to bring good luck. It has a design inspired by the flowers of the Post-Vegas World Tree, and is popular among tourists buying gifts.
Workers Daily - Special Issue A special issue of a newspaper run by a publishing company in Post-Vegas. It contains detailed information on the lack of investment capital available to Stockyard Guards.
Casino Chip A chip of the lowest denomination, used in the Post-Vegas casino. You will not be able to participate in games with only this as your stake.
Post-Vegas Tuna A large tuna fish that inhabits the ocean near Post-Vegas. The fatty part of the fish is extremely popular with tourists. There are some of its kind that rival a small boat in size.
ITO Sushi Wasabi A tube of wasabi paste, sourced from the riversides of the Departed Land. Its unique taste goes well with sushi, but caution is advised—even a small amount can add quite the kick.
Kraken Ink A vial of commercial kraken ink, collected from the excretions of the kraken. The inherent poison in the ink has been neutralized, making the product food-safe.
Gold Aloe A golden aloe plant that grows in the wastelands on the western edge of Post-Vegas. It's popular with sightseers, and is often used in beauty products and sunscreen.
Research Village Milk A local specialty from the Research Village, used in various recipes. It is an essential ingredient in the sweets that are currently popular in Post-Vegas.
Cliff Sugarcane A stalk of sugarcane, sourced near the Research Village's cliffs. It is commonly processed into high-grade sugar, which is then used to produce the village's sweets.
Circle Railway Drops A pack of multi-colored candies, stored in a train-shaped tin. It's a popular souvenir for the Circle Railway, and if you give it a shake, you can hear the sweets rattle inside.
Star Candy Crystals of sugar in the shapes of stars, enjoyed throughout generations. It takes skilled hands to form the perfect star shapes.
Research Village Cheese A block of cheese that has been produced in the Research Village's dairy farms since their inception. Goes well with a glass of Salvation Wine.
Resort Pearl (Flame Pattern) A rare pearl that has a unique flame-like pattern on its surface. If one were to use it as an accessory, it would draw the eyes of everyone around.
Resort Pearl A unique pearl with a pale luster, cultivated by Merchants in the sea by Post-Vegas. This particular shade is popular with ladies.
Bitter Jelly: Complete Nutrition A highly nourishing substance that contains all the nutrients you need for an entire day. Apparently, it has a distinctive taste that cannot be described.
Bitter Jelly: Vitamin Boost A lime-flavored jelly, developed in cooperation with the Miners. It contains a day's worth of vitamins, and is the frequent go-to for busy Merchants everywhere.
Rainbow Swallow's Nest A nest made by the vibrant-feathered rainbow swallow. Their nests were once over-hunted, severely reducing the population of the species itself.
Swallow's Nest A nest made by a swallow, a species that inhabits Post-Vegas. It's incredibly delicious in soups, and is considered a high-end ingredient.
Cursed Stone A stone that is said to drain life-force. It apparently grows redder and redder until it reaches crimson—at which point the owner loses their life... All that's a lie, though—it's just a rock made of obsidian.
Promise Braid A braid made out of tough, synthetic fibers. Apparently, the crews of the Frontier Vehicles would wear this as a good luck charm.
Model Kit: Floating Continent Armaments Series - Powered Suit (1/10 Scale) A model kit for a powered suit. It is part of the Floating Continent Armaments Series, and is popular with collectors fond of ordinance.
Frontier Vehicle Model A model of the large vehicles that were constructed during the Frontier Age. Legend has it that large numbers of people would ride these through corrupted land.
Paradise Pill A pill prepared by the Merchant Union. Taking it sharpens your senses and increases your concentration. However, moderation is key when taking medicines of this sort.
Seaside Hotel Projector A projector that is in every room of the Seaside Hotel. It can display media sent from video equipment.
Resort Seafood Extract A stock made from boiled down seafood. It's an essential flavoring in a multitude of dishes, causing it to be in high demand in other countries, too.
Stock Certificate: Abra Trading Company A stock certificate for the Post-Vegas-based Abra Trading Company. The group went under after its shares were taken by the Nile Trading Company a few decades ago—which means this item doesn't offer any claim to ownership.
Resort Fishing License A license that proves you have been given the right to fish by the ITO Foods group. Every fisherman brings this with them, but as it's small, many end up being misplaced while at sea.
Dragon Tarot Card -Breath- A tarot card associated with the Draconic Faith. It depicts a fierce dragon bellowing out torrents of flame—a sign good luck is coming your way, apparently.
Treasure Record of Fernando, the King of Adventurers A memo left by the Treasure Hunter known as the "King of Adventurers" on his deathbed. It contains information on a secret treasure on the Floating Continent that he spent his entire life searching for.
Creepy Bisque Doll A bisque doll that's often referred to as a cursed doll. There are endless rumors of it moving at night, its hair growing, and it bleeding out of its eyes.
Souvenir Coin: Post-Vegas Tower A limited-edition coin, issued to commemorate the completion of the Post-Vegas Tower. It rarely appears on the market, which has driven up its price among collectors.
Relic -Broken Watch- An ancient timepiece relic discovered in the Ocean Ruins that is broken and no longer moves. The name Antoine Philippe can be seen inscribed into the rim if inspected more closely.
