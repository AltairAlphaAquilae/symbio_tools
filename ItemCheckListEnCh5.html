Bio-Killer Can An empty spray can found by hunters in the deep forest. The side bears a strange symbol and the words "Bio-Killer."
Gas Mask A gas mask commonly owned by Miners. Used in hazardous zones such as laboratories where toxic chemicals are present or areas with poisonous gas emissions.
Alkaline Power Liquid A water-based alkaline solution frequently used in relic research. It is applied to remove stubborn oil stains clinging to ancient artifacts.
Centennial Spirit Tree Charm A charm crafted from a Spirit Tree that has lived for over a hundred years. Since they are made in alignment with the tree's growth, only a few are produced each year, making them highly valuable.
Bridal Bouquet A wedding essential that enhances the beauty of the bride. Created as a symbol of eternal love and a blissful future for the couple.
Zodiac Constellation Dial A star chart used during the Six Power Era, displaying the twelve zodiac constellations. The back is engraved with symbolic illustrations representing each constellation.
Stone Arm A blackened arm wrapped in tattered bandages. Some believe it was sculpted by a deranged artist in the likeness of their lover's arm.
Wedding Cake (Departed Land & Land of Salvation Special) A wedding cake made with the finest fruits from both the Departed Land and the Land of Salvation. A master patissier's proud masterpiece.
Jungle Special A lunchbox packed with Departed Land delicacies. A favorite among those venturing into the jungle.
Gold Chess Piece of the Undersecretary A chess piece modeled after the Undersecretary. Handcrafted by artisans of the time to honor his contributions to the Floating Continent migration, making it a rare collectible.
Departed Land Calvados A high-end brandy distilled in the Departed Land's Floating Utopia. Its refined taste and rich apple aroma make it so sought after that there is often a years-long waiting list for new shipments.
Fable's Holy Staff A relic from the Six Power Era. It differs in shape from the sacred staves currently used by the Departed Land's military. The name Fable is engraved on its shaft.
Tonic of Eternal Life An herbal medicine concocted by the Sprites since ancient times. Though it is said to grant immortality, no definitive proof of such effects has ever been found.
Arachne Cement An adhesive that clings with immense strength. Made from the abundant secretions of a giant spider, it is widely favored by carpenters and furniture makers across nations.
Baby's Breath Earrings Earrings shaped like the baby's breath flower, which is said to have once existed on Terrestria. Though it does not grow on the Floating Continent, it holds great significance for Sprites.
Ritual Craftsman's Rosary A ceremonial artifact crafted by a Sprite artisan. Devout believers always carry one. Its exquisite craftsmanship gives it artistic value as well.
Starlight Gold Granules Large gold particles collected from Departed Land rivers. Their high reflectivity makes them shine as if they were glowing on their own.
Placer Gold Dust Tiny gold particles often found in the riverbeds of the Departed Land. While not as valuable as Starlight Gold, a patient collector can amass a respectable fortune with these.
Wedding Magazine: Celebration (Collector's Edition) A special issue created by the Lewis Wedding Company to promote marriage culture on the Floating Continent. The cover features a Sprite-Normie couple.
Empty Medicine Bottle An empty bottle that once contained an unknown substance. A small amount of liquid residue clings to the bottom, making it unwise to touch with bare hands.
Black Gunpowder An explosive used in mining operations within the Floating Utopia's coal pits. Though powerful enough to shatter mountains, miners tend to handle it carelessly.
Compact Bow: Raptor A foldable bow designed to hunt the deadly beasts of the deep forest. By switching arrow types, it can adapt to any target.
Holy Fire Arrow A sacred weapon that traps fire within a protective barrier. Upon release, the barrier shatters mid-air, unleashing flames across a wide area.
Holy Arrow One of the sacred weapons developed in the Departed Land. Its arrowhead is encased in a barrier, vastly increasing its piercing power. Effective not only in combat but also against large-scale war machines.
Broken Music Box A battery-operated music box. Due to a drained battery, it emits distorted, fragmented sounds, making the original melody unrecognizable.
Used Holy Battery A depleted power source once used in sacred weapons. It seems to retain a small amount of energy.
River Fishing Rod: The Devourer A fishing rod favored by anglers who fish in the deep forest. Sturdy enough to withstand the power of giant piranhas.
Departed Land Detox Brew A medicinal tea made by slowly decocting dried herbs native to the Departed Land. Effective against natural poisons from insects, fungi, and other toxic organisms.
Departed Land Bee Larva A rare delicacy harvested from the apiaries of the Floating Utopia. Packed with nutrients, though its appearance often leads it to be considered a bizarre food.
Poetry Book: Requiem A book filled with handwritten text. Though written in Terrestrian script, the ink has smudged over time, making it difficult to read.
Miner-Made Flashbang A prototype flash grenade developed by the Miners. When thrown at a beast, it temporarily blinds them, making it a promising defensive weapon.
Guardian Spirit Model: Cancer A model of the guardian spirit Cancer that existed in the past. Known for its mastery of barrier manipulation, it was said to have protected many from all manner of attacks.
Relic -Centillion's Answer- An acoustic device once sold by the now-defunct corporate giant Centillion. When spoken to, it responds ever so slightly.
Godspring Gem A rare gemstone found in the rivers of the Departed Land. Formed over centuries and imbued with World Tree energy.
Ripe Mandrake A fully grown mandrake capable of emitting ultrasonic waves that sap the will to fight. Prolonged exposure can be fatal.
Unripe Mandrake A partially grown mandrake sometimes used as an ingredient in special medicines. When uprooted, it emits a faint, voice-like sound.
Spider Queen's Thread A web spun across the territory of a giant spider. Extremely durable and adhesive. Once stuck, it is nearly impossible to remove.
Giant Fang Fossil A massive fossilized fang, roughly the size of a human torso. Scholars have determined that it belonged to a giant wolf species that still lives on the Floating Continent.
Unrestored Painting A deteriorated painting that has withstood the test of time. The faintly visible structure in the background bears a resemblance to the Sanctuary of the Departed Land that is difficult to pin down to any one point.
Forest Hunter 45 The standard-issue firearm for treasure hunters in the deep forest. To prevent misfires in emergencies, revolvers have remained their weapon of choice for years.
Superior Cliff Cacao A premium variety of cliff cacao with an exceptionally strong aroma. It commands high prices on the market, making cliff cacao gatherers relentless in searching for it.
Cliff Cacao A type of cacao that grows on the sheer cliffs of the Floating Continent. Valued for its use in sweets and as a spice, it maintains a steady presence in the market at a reasonable price.
Natural Vanilla A fragrant plant found in the deep forest. ITO Foods has taken an interest in its scent and is rumored to be using it in the development of new desserts.
Red Rose A deep crimson rose that symbolizes true love. The most popular flower used in wedding bouquets.
Red Gerbera​ A pale red gerbera representing hope. Its tall, upright stem and radiant bloom exude wisdom and intelligence.
Founder Statue A stone effigy depicting the leader of the Church during the Six Power Era. Its dignified expression instills a sense of trust and authority.
Terrestrian Merchant's Parcel A package wrapped in Terrestrian creature leather. Once used by Merchants of the old world, it sometimes contains polished stones or uniquely shaped nuts.
Departed Land Berry A signature wild strawberry native to the deep forest. Known for its large size, refreshing acidity, and juiciness, it enjoys immense popularity both domestically and abroad.
Smoked Crocodile Meat The most popular dish at hunter taverns. Its rich, salty flavor intensifies the more it is chewed, making it the perfect item to pair with booze.
Giant Piranha Skewer A skewered giant piranha, a fearsome predator lurking in the rivers near the deep forest. Though it has little edible flesh due to its massive bones, the fatty meat is surprisingly delicious.
Whirlpool Beans An edible bean that grows wild in certain Departed Land forests. When boiled, it makes an excellent snack to accompany a strong drink. However, eaten raw, it is intensely bitter.
Caltrop Hip-Flask A flask beloved by nearly every veteran Guard with a taste for drinking. Even the cheapest liquor gains depth and complexity when stored in it, or so they claim.
Departed Land Spring Water Pristine water collected from the rivers of the Departed Land. Completely free of impurities, it is prized not only as drinking water but also for cooking.
Goldfish Scale A large scale from a legendary golden fish that exists only in the Land of Salvation. Said to bring great fortune to those who possess it.
Lock of Refined Hair A carefully groomed lock of hair, cut and dedicated by Sprites to the Grand Temple and Church Ruins. It is believed that well-maintained hair holds spiritual energy.
Floating Utopia Medicinal Soup A broth made from mountain vegetables and herbs found in the Floating Utopia and deep forest. The people of the Utopia are said to owe their vitality to their habit of drinking this soup.
Forest Cap A highly toxic mushroom potent enough to knock out even a Spike if eaten raw. However, with the right knowledge, it can be processed into a powerful healing ingredient.
Dragon Tarot Card -Ruin- A tarot card linked to the Draconic Faith. It depicts a world ravaged in the aftermath of the dragon's breath. A symbol of misfortune.
